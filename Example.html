About this Website

Welcome to the algebra website! This website contains everything you need to know to ace your tests, re-learn/practice your math skills, or to learn ahead. Regardless of your intentions, I hope this website provides you with the information you need. Simply choose a topic to learn about it and its various methods of solving. Find the right method that makes math easier and more fun for you! We hope to educate students all over the world on the ways of math! While many people may have a disliking for it, math is applied many times in real life and can be very useful. If you learn math now, you may thank yourself later in life when the knowledge comes in handy. This website also provides practice and worksheets for you to fill out. Enjoy the website and learn something!
